Pi of things

A framework to help bring together all your IoT projects

Setup

  • Setup your own website, following the simple instructions (coming soon).
  • Create an account
  • Register and manage your IoT devices online.

Register your Pi

Register each device you may have and install software modules as you need.

Once installation is complete play around with the various options.
Today we are ready to help you connect to your GPIO ports to light LEDs, flip relays or communicate with devices that use GPIO.
Soon we hope to able to support the whole gamut of Pi extensibility.
The aim is to provide you with a kickstart using your Pi. After that sky's the limit.

Tweak and have fun

All the code for Pi of things is open source and free, fork the code, tune it as you like it and have your own setup!!!
